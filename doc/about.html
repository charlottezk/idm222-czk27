Hi, I'm Charlotte.
Charlotte Khamnei is a user experience design, interface design, and software engineering student at Drexel University. She is currently based in West Philadelphia and is working remotely as a web-design consultant for the Mad River Valley Chamber of Commerce.
Back in Vermont, where she grew up, Charlotte was introduced to visual storytelling (and digital media) through a journalism program for high-school students (Conversations From The Open Road), for whom she would eventually intern for. Due to her extensive background in working with youth of all ages and in communications, Charlotte was able take on a wide range of roles, including:
collecting/organizing footage for each 2-week-long trip (~10 interviews each), mentoring trip participants in conducting interviews and creating independent documentaries shorts, and coordinating trip logistics and funding.
Most recently, Charlotte sought out freelance opportunities while being stuck at home due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Her work with the MRVCC has gained her valuable experience navigating remote consulting. Her deep history with the Mad River Valley (see resume) and expertise in user experience design has helped in optimizing the usability of the MRVCC's web services.
